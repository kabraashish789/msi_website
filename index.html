Guaranteed Purity

No guesswork. Just verified metal grades.

Mixed or contaminated scrap can lead to production issues, inconsistent alloys, and unnecessary losses. That's why every lot is carefully inspected, tested, and sorted before it leaves our facility.

  • Handheld XRF Metal Analysis

    Every batch is tested using a handheld XRF analyzer to verify the metal grade and chemical composition, ensuring accurate material identification before dispatch.

  • Rigorous Manual Sorting

    Our experienced team carefully removes mixed metals, iron contamination, attachments, and non-metallic impurities to deliver cleaner, furnace-ready scrap.

  • Transparent Quality Assurance

    Every shipment includes accurate weight documentation, and XRF test reports can be provided upon request for complete confidence in the supplied material.
