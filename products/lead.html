Lead Scrap — All Grades

Lead is one of the most completely recycled metals — over 80% of all lead comes from recycled sources. We supply clean soft lead, battery scrap, and cable sheathing grades with full compliance documentation.

Pb 60–99%+5 Grades Available80%+ Recycled ContentEXIM Compliant

5 Lead Scrap Grades

Lead scrap purity ranges from ultra-clean soft lead sheets to complex battery scrap that contains plastics and acid. Proper grading and documentation are critical for lead — all our lead scrap comes with required environmental and regulatory clearances.

Soft Lead Scrap
Also known as: Pure Lead / Lead Sheets
Pb 99%+

Soft Lead is the highest purity lead scrap — clean, unalloyed lead from sheeting, roofing, radiation shielding, and cable sheathing. It is very dense, extremely malleable, and among the most completely recyclable of all metals.

Typical Composition
Lead (Pb) 99.5%
Antimony (Sb) 0.3%
Other 0.2%
Common Applications
Battery manufacturingRadiation shieldingCable sheathingLead pipeBallast weights
Enquire for Soft Lead Scrap
Battery Scrap Lead
Also known as: ULAB / Whole Battery Scrap
Pb 60–75%

Battery Scrap refers to used lead-acid batteries from automotive, industrial, and telecom applications. The entire battery contains lead plates, lead oxide paste, and sulphuric acid. The most abundant source of secondary lead globally — over 80% of all lead produced is from recycled batteries.

Typical Composition
Lead (Pb) — Plates & Grid 60%
Lead Oxide Paste 20%
Polypropylene Case 10%
Acid / Other 10%
Common Applications
Lead acid battery re-manufacturingSecondary lead smeltingLead oxide for batteries
Enquire for Battery Scrap Lead
Lead Plates
Also known as: Battery Plates / Lead Grid Scrap
Pb 85–95%

Lead Plates are the grid and plate components removed from disassembled batteries. They have a higher lead purity than whole battery scrap since the casing and acid have been removed. Widely traded as a clean, dense, high-value lead scrap grade.

Typical Composition
Lead (Pb) 90%
Lead Oxide 7%
Antimony (Sb) 2%
Other 1%
Common Applications
Secondary lead smeltingBattery re-manufacturingLead alloy making
Enquire for Lead Plates
Cable Sheathing Lead
Also known as: Lead Cable Scrap / Telephone Lead
Pb 98–99%

Lead cable sheathing comes from old telephone, power, and data cables where lead was used as an outer protective layer. It is a very clean, high-purity lead scrap. Increasingly available as old cable infrastructure is retired and replaced.

Typical Composition
Lead (Pb) 98.5%
Tin (Sn) 0.8%
Bismuth (Bi) 0.5%
Other 0.2%
Common Applications
High-purity lead re-meltRadiation shieldingLead alloysChemical lead
Enquire for Cable Sheathing Lead
Lead Dross
Also known as: Lead Skimmings / Lead Oxide Dross
Pb 60–80%

Lead dross is the oxide-rich material skimmed from the surface of molten lead during smelting or refining. It has significant lead content but requires further processing. A normal byproduct of any lead recycling or smelting operation.

Typical Composition
Lead (Pb) 70%
Lead Oxide (PbO) 20%
Antimony 5%
Other 5%
Common Applications
Secondary lead refiningLead oxide productionRecycling feedstock
Enquire for Lead Dross
