Sales & Marketing

Business Development Executive

Jamnagar, Gujarat Full-time

Identify and develop new buyer relationships for our brass, copper, aluminium, and zinc scrap grades across domestic markets.

Responsibilities

  • Generate and follow up on leads for sorted scrap grades and extruded rods
  • Build relationships with foundries, manufacturers, and traders
  • Coordinate with the sourcing team on grade availability and pricing
  • Represent the company at industry exhibitions and buyer visits

Requirements

  • 1–4 years of experience in metals, scrap, or industrial B2B sales
  • Strong communication skills in Gujarati, Hindi, and English
  • Willingness to travel for client visits and exhibitions

Export Sales Manager

Jamnagar, Gujarat Full-time

Lead export enquiries for our precision components division, building relationships with international buyers in electrical, sanitary, valve, automotive, and sensor industries.

Responsibilities

  • Manage end-to-end export enquiries — quoting, documentation, coordination
  • Build and maintain relationships with overseas buyers
  • Track EXIM compliance and shipping documentation
  • Work with production to align capacity with export commitments

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience in export sales, preferably in metals or components
  • Familiarity with EXIM documentation and international trade terms
  • Excellent written and verbal English

Operations & Production

CNC / VMC Machine Operator

Jamnagar, Gujarat Full-time

Operate CNC and VMC machines producing precision brass components for electrical, sanitary, valve, automotive, and sensor industries.

Responsibilities
