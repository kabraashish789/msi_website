Our Story & Legacy

Since 1979, Metal Scrap India has been supplying quality non-ferrous metal scrap with integrity, consistency, and a commitment to responsible recycling. Today, we continue to serve manufacturers and foundries across India through trusted domestic and global sourcing.

Our Story

Four Decades of Growth

From a small trading outfit to a Pan-India name in non-ferrous metal recycling — here's how our journey unfolded.

1979

The Beginning

We started out as Sunrise Metal, from a modest 2,500 sq. ft. facility, we handled 80–100 tonnes of scrap every month while building long-term relationships across the industry.

2000

Expansion into Manufacturing

We expanded beyond trading and launched two new ventures — Shree Ram Foundry and Shree Shyam Foundry — dedicated to manufacturing brass sand casting rods.

2002

A New Identity

Sunrise Metal was rebranded as Metal Scrap India, reflecting our growing presence and long-term vision within the non-ferrous metal industry.

2008

Launch of Manglam Metals

We set up Manglam Metals, a dedicated brass extrusion manufacturing plant spanning 12,000 sq. ft. with a production capacity of 80 tons per month.

2019

Expanding Our Operations

Metal Scrap India moved into a larger 24,000 sq. ft. facility, scaling our handling capacity up to 140 tons per month.

Present & Beyond

Continuing the Legacy

Today, Metal Scrap India sources premium non-ferrous metal scrap from trusted suppliers across the world while continuing to serve manufacturers and foundries throughout India. Backed by expanded infrastructure, modern testing facilities, and more than four decades of experience, we remain committed to delivering reliable quality and dependable service.
